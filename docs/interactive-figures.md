# Interactive Figures

This page embeds interactive versions of the key figures from the paper
*"ML-guided screening of chalcogenide perovskites as solar energy materials"*.

The figures are generated by running:

```bash
python docs/generate_interactive_figures.py
```

from the repository root. Output HTML files land in
`docs/assets/figures/` (CDN-linked Plotly, requires an internet
connection to render).

## Figure 6 — Pareto front: Supply Risk vs bandgap deviation

Multi-objective evaluation of material sustainability (Supply Risk, SR) versus
deviation of CrabNet-predicted bandgap from the optoelectronic optimum.

- **Red triangles** — Pareto-optimal candidates (minimum SR *and* minimum
  |E_g^ − E_g^opt|)
- **Blue squares** — materials within 10 % of both minima
- **Gray circles** — remaining candidates

Hover over any point to see the formula, bandgap, and SR.
